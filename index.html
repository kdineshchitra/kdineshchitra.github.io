About Me


I am a thriving Data scientist and a machine learning enthusiast. I am currently developing my skills and knowledge in the world of data. I desire to learn the complete workflow of this data-driven world and to be a part of it. I started my journey by learning python. I have done some data analysis, preprocessing, visualization and machine learning model building using Python libraries. Currently, I am learning SQL.

Why Data Science?
Initially, I chose this buzzword intending to make money. But after enough research about the topic, I found myself enjoying learning machine learning. Now, I am one of the people who believe data is the key to the future. I like to think that manipulating data and extracting insights from information and solving real-world problems will lead humans to create a better world. And one more thing is the fun when you command machines to learn themselves for us and to make our life easier. I have come to know that I cannot completely learn everything in the DS/ML sector and that intrigues me to pursue more. Which means I will never stop educating myself.

How do I learn?
I use free/open-source internet resources, other expertsâ€™ works I find online to develop my skills. I learn daily and I learn a lot by doing. I have completed an online video course to get to know Python. I have learned a lot by solving problems and writing snippets on my own. I am ready to work, collaborate and learn with others. I want to contribute to open-source. If you are also trying to master the data and you wish to collaborate, do ping me. Let's see what we can find interesting to study and expand our knowledge together.

What is my goal?
I want to become a full-blown end-to-end Data Scientist, who can use the modern digital world's data resources to solve real-world problems and make everyone's life better. I like to think I am a lifelong learner. Because that is true. You cannot just finish learning and say I have learnt everything. My motto is "Living is learning; Learning is living". We grow our knowledge every day by exploring new things and we grow faster by collaborating with fellow learners.

What is other than data?
I am from Tamilnadu, India. I have completed my bachelor's degree in chemical engineering in Anna University, Chennai. I play football and handball. I have been the college handball team goalkeeper for 2 years. I read Tamil fictions, author Sujatha is my favourite.
