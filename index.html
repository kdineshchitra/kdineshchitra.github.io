About Me

Hello there! You probably noticed my name somewhere on this site. I am from Tamilnadu, India. I have neither professional status nor work/job experience to display in this section. I don’t have a proper computer science degree or similar. That is me, that is what I am doing, trying to become a Data Scientist without a college degree.

So, Who am I now? - A self-learning data science aspirant.

  • What do I mean by Self-learning? - I use free/open-source internet resources, other experts’ works I find online.
  • What do I mean by Data Science? - mostly ML work, exploratory data analysis, data visualization and other things I think are data scientists’ jobs.
  • What do I mean by aspirant? - A Noob.

I am a person seeking a stable/proper career in the world of data. I have a significant amount of passion to become a data scientist. I have a desire to learn the complete workflow of this data-driven world and to be a part of it.

Why Data Science? - I chose this buzzword intending to make money. But after enough research about the topic, I found myself enjoying learning machine learning. Now, I am one of the people who believe data is the key to the future. I like to think that manipulating data and extracting insights from information and solving real-world problems will lead humans to create a better world. And one more thing is the fun when you command machines to learn for us and to make our life easier. I have come to know that I cannot completely learn everything in the DS/ML sector and that intrigues me to learn more. Which means I will never stop learning.

Some points to be noted,

  • I am not a fast learner but I learn daily and I learn a lot by doing.
  • I am ready to work, collaborate and learn with others.
  • I want to contribute to open-source.
  • I am not a native English speaker (so kindly ignore the grammatical & contextual errors, if any).
  • I want to teach others in my native language (which is Tamil-தமிழ்)
